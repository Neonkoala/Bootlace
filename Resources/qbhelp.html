What is QuickBoot?
QuickBoot allows you to reboot the device directly into the selected operating system or the console without affecting your normal OpeniBoot settings. This means when you next reboot the device OpeniBoot will revert to its usual settings and boot into your usual OS or wait for you to pick one depending on your settings.
How do I use it?
Simply tap the OS you wish to boot into and after a confirmation your device will reboot directly into the selected OS with no intervention.
